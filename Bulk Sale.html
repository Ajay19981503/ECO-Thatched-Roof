Bulk Order FAQs

Common questions about our bulk bamboo materials

What is the minimum order quantity for bulk purchases?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product. For bamboo poles, the minimum is 500 units. For paneling and flooring, the minimum is 200 square meters. Please contact us for specific MOQs for other products.

Do you offer international shipping for bulk orders?

Yes, we regularly ship bulk orders internationally. We handle all export documentation and can arrange both sea and air freight. Shipping costs will be calculated based on your location and order volume.

Can I get custom sizes for bulk orders?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom sizing for bulk orders. Our production facilities can create bamboo materials to your exact specifications. Minimum order quantities may apply for custom sizes.

What certifications do your bamboo products have?

Our bamboo materials are certified by the Forest Stewardship Council (FSC), ISO 9001 for quality management, and meet international building code standards. We also provide additional certifications upon request.

How long does production take for bulk orders?

Standard bulk orders ship within 15-30 days after order confirmation. For very large orders (over 10,000 units) or custom products, production may take 45-60 days. We provide a detailed timeline with your quote.
