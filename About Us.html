Discover Our Story

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by Dilip Sahni, GreenBamboo Homes started as a small workshop with a big vision. Growing up in a rural village surrounded by bamboo groves, Dilip witnessed firsthand the incredible potential of this renewable resource. Frustrated by the environmental impact of conventional construction, he set out to create sustainable alternatives.

Our journey began with a single bamboo hut and a commitment to eco-friendly practices. What started as a passion project has grown into an innovative company with projects spanning across three countries and a team of dedicated bamboo specialists.

Today, we blend traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering to create structures that are not only beautiful but also environmentally responsible. Each project we undertake reflects our core values of sustainability, quality, and innovation.
